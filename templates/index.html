About Our Portal

For any evaluation process of applicant/ employee/ student/ candidate it is essential to get analytics for better decision making. Data analytics and intelligence has been proving to be critical tool for the decision makes.

When candidate is being evaluated by institute , it is important to get analytics which can help to take decisions about the candidate including his/her strong weak areas, improvements needed, overall analysis and if candidate is suitable or not. If assessment is linked with analytics decision bias associated with selection process or evaluation process can be reduced.

Alphabots is an Online Examination Platform used by many universities, Professional Training Institutes, Colleges to manage their Entrance Exams, internal assessments of the students. Currently more than 100,000 exams have been conducted on this platform. It is cloud based platform with autoscale facility to manage examinations of any scale.
